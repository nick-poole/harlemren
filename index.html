Embracing Legacy

At the intersection of tradition and trend, The New Harlem Renaissance emerges as a beacon of grooming excellence, embodying the spirit of the historic Harlem Renaissance in every cut, shave, and trim.

Built on the foundational values of precision, craftsmanship, and client satisfaction, The New Harlem Renaissance is a celebration of style, culture, and individuality. Here, each service is a homage to the timeless sophistication of the Harlem Renaissance, providing clientele with not just superior grooming services, but an opportunity to partake in a rich cultural legacy.

The Revival of Style

In 1920s Harlem, a cultural revolution was taking place, showcasing the vibrancy of Black culture and influencing everything from music and literature to fashion and style. Within this vibrant period, barbershops emerged as communal hubs, where styles weren't merely followed, but passionately created, serving as powerful symbols of African American entrepreneurship and self-expression.

Drawing inspiration from this iconic era, this brand sets itself apart by fusing old-world artistry with contemporary barbering techniques, creating a unique experience that caters to the modern, style-conscious individual while honoring the past.

The New Harlem Renaissance invites you to step into a world where the past and present converge, where style meets heritage. Experience the meticulous attention to detail, personalized services, and unmatched expertise that defines the brand. The New Harlem Renaissance isn't just about looking good, it's about feeling connected to a vibrant history while standing out in the modern world.

Kennard Perry, a NYC native and graduate of the esteemed Morgan State University, is the vision and drive behind The New Harlem Renaissance. Perry earned his Bachelor of Arts in Political Science and Government in 1997, but it was his passion for barbering that led him to create something truly unique. He currently manages the Nile Style Barbershop in Northeast Baltimore, bringing his vision and expertise to clients daily.

Perry sees the New Harlem Renaissance as a revival of the industry's core values: education, style, and unrivaled customer service. It echoes the era when a well-groomed man signified not only sophistication but was also a reservoir of knowledge, wisdom personified in the barber's chair. This is a return to the roots, placing the client at the heart of the industry once again. It is a tribute to the time when barbershops were not just businesses, but cultural epicenters of the community. Here, style is not merely a display of skills but also a testament to the barber's personal flair. It's the renaissance of the gentleman barber, a profession grounded in artistry and finesse.

Services

Reviving the Art of Grooming

Discover a wide spectrum of grooming services, customized to meet the needs of adults, teens, and children. Each service, from the basic cuts to intricate designs, is designed with the aim of making every client look and feel their best. Specialized treatments such as undercuts, shape-ups, and artistic hair designs add a unique personal touch.

For a more comprehensive grooming experience, consider the uniquely named experiences. These experiences, each reflecting the spirit of the Harlem Renaissance, range from 'The Classic Harlem' to the 'Self-Care Sanctuary'. They provide a blend of classic and modern grooming techniques to cater to individual style and grooming needs. Services extend beyond hair care to include enhancements such as hairline and beard adjustments, eyebrow arching, and beard sculpting & trimming. A selection of signature services is also on offer. These include hair bump removal, premium facial treatments involving steam, scrub, mask, and extraction, and specialized scalp care involving scrub and exfoliation. Explore these offerings to discover the perfect blend of style, comfort, and sophistication.

The Classic Harlem

A nod to tradition, this category offers timeless grooming services. It can include basic services such as a haircut, shape up, and beard trim.

The Visionary

For those who are not afraid to stand out, this tier takes it up a notch with unique personal expressions. Services include everything from the Classic Harlem tier, plus creative hair designs and hairline and beard enhancements.

The Gentleman

The epitome of sophistication. This tier includes everything from the Visionary tier, plus holistic grooming experiences like a steam facial, beard wash, shape and sculpt, and a scalp scrub.

The Renaissance Man

The all-encompassing experience, mirroring the comprehensive nature of the Harlem Renaissance itself. This higher tier includes every service from the Gentleman tier, along with exclusive services like beard sculpting, scalp scrub and exfoliation, and hairline and beard enhancements.

The Self Care Sanctuary

The ultimate grooming experience designed to provide complete relaxation and rejuvenation. This top tier includes all services from the Renaissance Man, and can go even further by including services dedicated to personal wellbeing such as hair bump removal, steam facial mask scrub, and shaved head services.

Appointments

Seamless Booking for Impeccable Grooming

Embark on your grooming journey with The New Harlem Renaissance. The online booking system makes it simple and convenient for you to secure your appointment. With just a few clicks, you can choose your preferred services, select a time slot, and have your appointment confirmed.

To ensure a seamless experience, please take note of the following:

  • Arrive a few minutes early to allow for a prompt start.
  • If you're unable to make your appointment, please cancel at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Missed appointments or late cancellations may be subject to a fee.
  • Your punctuality, cooperation, and understanding help ensure the best service to all clients.
  • Appointments are currently held at The Nile Style Barbershop in Northeast Baltimore. The Nile Style is located at the intersection of Northern Pkwy & Harford Road.

Please note that any additional services requested during your appointment will incur additional charges. You'll be informed of these prior to service delivery, ensuring there are no surprises when it comes time for payment. Timely communication about any additional services you'd like to add helps in providing a smooth and enjoyable experience. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact Kennard.
